1. Who is a prospective member?

When you sign up (or are approved from a Join application), you become a prospective member. You get access to the member portal so you can take part in club life — attend events, read announcements, browse the directory and resources — while you build up the participation needed to become a full member.

The only things a prospective member doesn't have yet: you're not listed in the public member directory until you're promoted to full member.

2. Creating your account & logging in

1
Go to Sign Up (or click Join Us and create an account).
2
Enter your name, email, and a password.
3
You're signed in and taken straight to your member dashboard.

Next time, log in at Sign In. Forgot your password? Use the Forgot password link to reset it by email.

3. Your dashboard

Your dashboard is your home base. At the top you'll find:

  • Check in to an event — scan an event's QR code or type its code (see §4).
  • Your path to membership — a progress card showing the requirements and how many you've completed (see §5).
  • Announcements and upcoming events.

4. Checking in to events (QR)

Your attendance is how you progress toward membership, so check in at every event you attend.

1
On your dashboard (or the Attendance page) tap Scan QR.
2
Point your camera at the event's QR code displayed at the venue.
3
No camera? Type the event code shown beside the QR into the box and tap Go.
You'll see a confirmation when you're checked in. If an event has already ended, check-in is closed — ask an admin to record your attendance manually.

5. Your path to membership

The club sets how much participation is required — for example, a number of Club Meetings, Fellowships, Projects, etc. Your dashboard progress card shows each requirement with a dot that turns green when you've met it.

On the cardWhat it means
Grey dot, e.g. "Club meetings 1/4"Still in progress — keep attending.
Green dotThat requirement is complete.
"All requirements met" bannerYou're ready — an admin will confirm your membership.

6. The rest of the portal

My Profile

Add your photo, occupation, and contact details.

My Events

Browse upcoming club events so you know what to attend.

Announcements

Read the latest club-wide messages.

Directory

Browse current members and search by name or occupation.

Resources

Download documents shared with the club.

7. Becoming a full member

Once your progress card shows all requirements met, an admin reviews and promotes you to full Member. From then on you also appear in the member directory — nothing else about how you use the portal changes.

8. Using the portal on your phone

The portal works on any phone. Tap the menu (☰) icon at the top-left to open navigation (Dashboard, Profile, Events, Attendance, Announcements, Directory, Resources). The QR check-in card on your dashboard uses your phone camera directly.
