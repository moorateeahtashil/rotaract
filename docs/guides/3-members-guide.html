1. Logging in

Go to Sign In, enter your email and password, and you'll land on your member dashboard. Use Forgot password to reset by email if needed. Sign out anytime from the My Account menu (top-right) or the side menu.

2. Your dashboard

The dashboard summarises your club at a glance:

  • Check in to an event card — scan or enter an event code (see §3).
  • Quick stats — upcoming events, your service hours, projects joined.
  • Announcements and upcoming events lists.

3. Checking in to events (QR)

1
On your dashboard (or the Attendance page) tap Scan QR.
2
Point your camera at the event's QR code at the venue — you're checked in instantly.
3
No camera available? Type the event code into the box and tap Go.
If an event has already ended, check-in is closed. Speak to an organiser/admin who can record attendance manually.

4. My Profile

Open My Profile to update your photo, occupation, company, and contact details. You can also control whether you appear in the member directory. Changes save immediately.

5. My Events

My Events lists upcoming club events with date, time, and location so you can plan what to attend (and check in when you're there).

6. Announcements

Announcements shows club-wide messages, newest first. Higher-priority items are flagged so you don't miss anything important.

7. Member Directory

The Directory lists fellow members. Use the search box to filter by name, occupation, company, or classification — handy for connecting with people across the club.

8. Resources

Resources holds documents shared with members (guides, templates, bylaws). Click to download. Some resources are public; others are member-only.

9. On your phone

Everything works on mobile. Tap the menu (☰) icon at the top-left to open navigation. The dashboard's QR check-in uses your phone camera, and the directory search and all forms are touch-friendly.
