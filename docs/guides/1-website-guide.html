1. What you'll find here

The website is the public face of the club — open to everyone, no login required. Use it to learn who we are, see what we do, follow our latest events and projects, and get in touch or apply to become a member.

2. Getting around

The menu bar at the top links to every main area:

MenuWhere it takes you
HomeHighlights, upcoming events, featured projects, and an introduction to the club.
About ▾About Rotaract, About Rotary, Our Club, and Leadership.
MembersThe public member listing.
Service ▾Projects, Events, and the Avenues of Service.
NewsClub news & blog posts.
GalleryPhoto albums from club activities.
FAQAnswers to common questions.

Top-right you'll always find Sign In (for members) and a gold Join Us button.

3. Exploring the club

About

Hover About to choose: About Rotaract (what Rotaract is), About Rotary (the global Rotary family), Our Club (our story and details), and Leadership (the current board).

Avenues of Service

Under Service → Avenues of Service, see the areas of focus the club serves, each with its own page.

4. Events, Projects & News

Events

Open Service → Events for upcoming and past events. Click any event to see its full details — date, time, location, and description.

Projects

Service → Projects showcases community service initiatives. Click a project for its story, gallery, and impact.

News

News lists the latest posts. Use the search box to find a topic, and click a post to read it in full.

5. Gallery, Members & Leadership

Gallery — browse photo albums from events and projects. Members — see the public member listing. Leadership (under About) — meet the current board and their roles.

6. FAQ & Contact

FAQ

The FAQ page answers common questions (membership, events, leadership). Click a question to expand its answer.

Contact

The Contact page has a form — fill in your name, email, subject, and message, then submit. The team is notified and you'll get an acknowledgement by email.

7. Applying to join

1
Click the gold Join Us button (top-right) or open the Join page.
2
Complete the membership form — your details and why you'd like to join.
3
Submit. The membership team is notified and you'll receive a confirmation email.
Prefer to create an account right away? Use Sign Up — you'll get access to the member portal as a prospective member while your membership is finalized (see the Prospective Members guide).

8. Using the site on your phone

The website is fully mobile-friendly. On a phone, tap the menu (☰) icon at the top-right to open the navigation; tap any item to go there, and tap a section heading to expand its sub-links. Sign In and Join Us appear at the bottom of the mobile menu.
